About Looker Studio Reports

Looker Studio reports are used to transform data into actionable dashboards for SEO, PPC, ecommerce, and marketing performance.

SEO dashboards track rankings, traffic, and search visibility, while PPC dashboards focus on ROAS, CPA, and ad performance.

Ecommerce dashboards combine revenue, conversion rate, and customer acquisition metrics.

Agency and client reports streamline communication, while white-label options allow branding customization.

Automation and tools like Supermetrics enable scalable reporting workflows.
